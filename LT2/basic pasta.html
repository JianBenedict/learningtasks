Basic Pasta

Ingredients

  • Dried Pasta (any will work)
  • well water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese/Tuna/Both/None :)

Method

  1. Boil your pasta adding salt to the water for later on.
  2. While pasta is cooking grate your cheese/drain your tuna
  3. When the pasta is cooked add about half a tube of tomato puree,stirring well. to give you a passata if you want, and drain the pasta instead
  4. Then add the tuna if using any,stirring it in well.Leave for a minute
  5. Serve into a bowl and sprinkle lots of cheese on top :)
    6. Enjoy!
    This can be enjoyed as a simple tomato or pasta,or with cheese or tuna or both :)
